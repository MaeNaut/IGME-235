ENGAGE IN WWI

Battlefield 1 presents the various stories and fights of World War I, inspired by the historical events. Players are given the weapons and armored vehicles used in the war, and you will fight on the battlegrounds based on the actual fights of World War I.
This game includes realistic graphics and historical research, allowing you to explore the wartime period.
From the first-person perspective, charge into the battlefield and experience World War I.

NUMEROUS GAME MODES

The campaign with “unforgettable war stories” is not the only game mode in Battlefield 1. There are several multiplayer modes, some of which can take up to 64 players at a time.

REAL WEAPONS

The actual weapons from each combatant nation are present. Customize your weapon and sweep the battlefield.

CHARACTERISTIC CLASSES

A soldier is categorized into infantry, vehicle, and elite, and each category is subdivided into classes. Each class uses their specialized weapons and items to fulfill their assigned jobs.

BEHEMOTH IS EN ROUTE

The Behemoth is a unique and ultimate vehicle with dominant power and armor. It is given to the losing team to allow them to turn the table.
