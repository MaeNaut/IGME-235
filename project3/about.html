Fast Math

Game Description

The game will give two random mathematical formulas each round with a time limit.
The player has to choose the one with greater or lower value to complete the round.
The time limit becomes shorter as the round proceeds.
If the player chooses the wrong formula or time is over, they lose.

Aesthetics

The design will be super simple and abstract. Though, I will find out the best layout so that
the player will be able to read the question, formulas, and time as fast as possible.

Gameplay

The player just chooses between two formulas using left mouse click.
The first round will give a plenty of time for players to understand what's going on.
